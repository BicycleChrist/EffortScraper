Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Why should I use Unabated for NHL Odds?

Unabated allows you to line shop NHL odds. Line shopping is one of the most critical tools a bettor has in their arsenal and the Unabated Odds Screen makes it easier than ever to find edges for hockey odds across sportsbooks.

What do I get for NHL odds by signing up to Unabated?

When you sign-up for Unabated Essentials, you'll get access to line history of the NHL odds, select NHL market maker odds, the compare lines feature, and many other betting calculators to help with your NHL betting.

Unabated Premium gives you all of the above and also includes the all NHL market maker odds, the Unabated Line for each game, in-game odds, alternate line and derivative calculators, and much more.

Click here to learn more about Unabated membership.

How can I compare NHL odds from different sportsbooks?

When you sign-up for Unabated, you can select multiple NHL odds from the Odds Screen and we will automatically compare the value of the two lines. This can also be done through our Compare Lines Calculator tool.

What NHL odds are available for comparing through Unabated's NHL Odds Screen?

We offer NHL odds for the top sportsbooks, including FanDuel Sportsbook, DraftKings Sportsbook, BetMGM, BetOnline, Bovada, and many more.

Unabated subscribers will also gain access to additional market maker sportsbooks such as Circa Sports, and the Unabated Line, which is a vig-free, expertly blended, consensus line from market-making sportsbooks. This is a great way to identify what the sharp price is for a market without having to know which sportsbooks are the sharpest for each sport.
